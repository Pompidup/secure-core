# Privacy Policy - SecureCore

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Summary

SecureCore does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your documents are stored locally on your device and encrypted.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** SecureCore has no analytics, no tracking, no telemetry, and no network communication of any kind.

## Data Storage

- All documents are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored on your device
- Encryption keys are managed by the Android Keystore and never leave your device
- No plaintext copy of your documents is ever written to disk
- All data is stored in the app's private storage, inaccessible to other apps

## Data Transmission

**No data is transmitted to any server.** SecureCore operates entirely offline. There are no API calls, no cloud sync, and no external dependencies.

## Data Backup

Android cloud backup is intentionally disabled. Your documents are never included in Google Drive backups or device-to-device transfers.

## Data Deletion

- You can delete any document from within the app at any time
- When you uninstall the app, all data (encrypted documents, metadata, and encryption keys) is permanently and irrecoverably deleted by the Android operating system
- There is no way to recover data after uninstallation

## Third-Party Services

SecureCore does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

SecureCore does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the "Last updated" date at the top will be changed. Material changes will be communicated via the app's Play Store listing.
